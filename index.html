FAQ

Answers to common questions from operators and cultivation teams.

Does it run without internet?

Yes. Z0neControl is local-first and continues operating when Wi-Fi or cloud services go down.

How is this different from typical controllers?

We measure canopy-level conditions, log every action to outcomes, and avoid aggressive tuning.

What sensors and controllers are supported?

We integrate zone and canopy sensors plus standard HVAC, dehu, vent, and lighting controllers.

What are the safety guardrails?

Fail-safe stops, drift tolerance, max runtime limits, and manual override options are built in.

Is it for cannabis only?

No. The platform is being generalized for broader CEA applications where stability matters.

What does a beta look like?

We run a short install + calibration, then monitor stability metrics and report outcomes weekly.
